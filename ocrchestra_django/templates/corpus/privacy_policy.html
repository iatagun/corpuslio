This Privacy Policy explains how OCRchestra ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal data in accordance with the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK - Law No. 6698) and the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Your privacy is important to us. We are committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about how we use it.

3. Personal Data We Collect

3.1 Account Information

  • Username and email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile information (name, institution, role)
  • Account creation and last login dates

3.2 Usage Data

  • Documents you upload (including content and metadata)
  • Search queries and analysis history
  • Export history and download logs
  • IP addresses and access logs
  • Browser type and device information

3.3 Corpus Data

  • Text content uploaded to the platform
  • Annotations and linguistic analysis results
  • Tags and collection metadata

4. How We Use Your Data

4.1 Legal Basis

We process your data based on:

  • Your consent - when you create an account and accept our terms
  • Contract performance - to provide you with our services
  • Legitimate interests - to improve our platform and research
  • Legal obligations - to comply with Turkish and EU law

4.2 Purposes

  • Providing corpus access and linguistic analysis tools
  • Processing and storing your uploaded documents
  • Generating statistics and visualizations
  • Protecting personal data through anonymization
  • Enforcing rate limits and quotas
  • Improving platform functionality and user experience
  • Academic and linguistic research (anonymized data only)

5. Data Anonymization

KVKK Compliance: We use Named Entity Recognition (NER) to detect and anonymize personal data in corpus texts, including names, email addresses, phone numbers, and ID numbers.

You can:

  • Request anonymization of your uploaded documents
  • View anonymization reports showing masked entities
  • Download anonymized versions of your data

6. Your Rights (KVKK and GDPR)

You have the right to:

  1. Access - Request a copy of your personal data
  2. Rectification - Correct inaccurate data
  3. Erasure - Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  4. Restriction - Limit how we process your data
  5. Data Portability - Receive your data in machine-readable format
  6. Object - Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  7. Withdraw Consent - At any time, without affecting prior processing

To exercise these rights, visit your Privacy Dashboard.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • Account data: Until account deletion (+ 30 day grace period)
  • Uploaded documents: Until you delete them or close your account
  • Query logs: 2 years from creation
  • Export logs: 2 years from creation
  • Anonymized corpus data: May be retained for research purposes

8. Data Security

We protect your data using:

  • Encrypted passwords (hashing with salt)
  • HTTPS encryption for data transmission
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Watermarked exports to prevent unauthorized distribution

9. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Researchers: Anonymized corpus data only, with your consent
  • Service providers: Cloud hosting and email services (under data processing agreements)
  • Legal authorities: When required by law

10. International Transfers

If your data is transferred outside Turkey or the EU, we ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by data protection authorities
  • Your explicit consent

11. Cookies and Tracking

We use:

  • Essential cookies: For authentication and session management
  • Functional cookies: To remember your preferences
  • Analytics cookies: To improve our platform (anonymized data)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

12. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 18. If you are under 18, please obtain parental consent before creating an account.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
